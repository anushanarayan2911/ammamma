A Life of Service

“When beauty, talent, intellect, and service know no bounds, they touch millions of lives. Such a soul is not merely the beloved of a family, but a cherished gift to society.”

Our Tribute

Celebrating Her Life

Our mother, Dr T Seetha passed away peacefully at home on 16th July 2026.

Thank you for upholding us in your prayers as we bid farewell to a once in a generation celebrated teacher, extraordinary healer, inspiring mentor, lifelong pillar of support to her immediate and extended family - and above all, a beloved mother, sister, aunt and grandmother.

Last rites at 2:30pm on Thursday 23rd July 2026 at Vaikuntha Mahaprasthanam, Shaikpet, Whisper Valley, Jubilee Hills.

- Gita Nath & Sandhya Ramayya

Life Timeline

A Journey of Grace & Impact

  1. 1927

    Early Beginnings

    Born to a family of educators, instilling a lifelong devotion to learning, classical arts, and community service.

  2. 1974

    Medical Degree & Specialization

    Graduated top of her medical class with honors, specializing in pediatrics to care for the most vulnerable.

  3. 1982

    Establishing Her Practice

    Opened her family practice clinic, creating a patient-first haven known for accessible and deeply empathetic care.

  4. 1995

    Joining the Faculty

    Appointed Professor of Pediatrics. Over the next 25 years, she trained over a thousand future medical professionals.

  5. 2015

    Lifetime Achievement

    Received the Lifetime Medical Excellence award for her outstanding contributions to pediatric public health.

  6. 2026

    A Peaceful Farewell

    Passed away peacefully in her sleep, leaving behind an indelible blueprint of a life magnificently lived.
